The Canadian-Muslim Vote

The Canadian-Muslim Vote is a national, non-partisan, educational organization whose aim is to increase Muslim civic engagement. I led the redesign of the two main educational sections of the Canadian-Muslim Vote's website, incorporating various elements such as videos and images.

The educational content within the site was very text heavy, and I realized early on that I had to do things to add to the readability of the text. So, I decided to do the following:

  • Highlight important parts of the text using strong, bold font (see Post Election Results Section pictured and here)
  • Visually arange text so that it is easy to digest (see speech from the throne section here)
  • Hide text (when possible) and give users the option to show it (see MP Mandate Letters Section here)
